## Dataset Overview

- **Ethereum Transaction Records:** ETGraph encompasses an extensive collection
  of Ethereum transaction records, capturing a significant portion of the
  Ethereum network's activities. These records include transaction hashes, block
  numbers, timestamps, sender and recipient addresses, transaction values, and
  more.

- **Twitter Data Integration:** In addition to Ethereum transaction records,
  ETGraph incorporates Twitter data, allowing users to analyze the correlation
  between social media activities and blockchain transactions. This integration
  opens up new avenues for research and analysis.

- **Unparalleled Scale:** ETGraph stands out as the largest dataset of its kind,
  offering an unparalleled scale of Ethereum transactions and associated Twitter
  data. This scale empowers users to conduct comprehensive analyses and gain
  deep insights into the dynamics of the Ethereum ecosystem.
